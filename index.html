About / Ray

The person who answers
is the person who arrives.

Ray operates Keygen Locksmiths from Bedford. There's no office taking the call and farming the job out to a stranger. The number on this site is Ray's mobile. He answers it, drives to you, and does the work himself.

Most jobs are everyday ones — lockouts, snapped keys, worn cylinders, UPVC mechanisms that have given up. Heritage locks, safes, and commercial jobs come through the same number. If it's outside what he carries, he'll say so rather than guess.

Check the reviews. "Honest" is the word that shows up more than any other — more than fast, more than professional. That tends to happen when there's nobody in the middle marking the job up.
